Project Overview

(Brief overview of the what the project is and what problem it is solving)

Our project is about getting CSGO game data of that user(kills, deaths, headshot percentage, inventory etc) through steam and display them on our website. In addition, players can also go through our market page to view and buys CSGO weapon skins, knifes, and gloves!

Project Architecture

(Outline of project architecture and technology used)

Our project is using MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular, Node) as the architecture
